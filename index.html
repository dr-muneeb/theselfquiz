Frequently Asked Questions

How does the "Create Your Own Quiz" feature work?

It transforms your text into an interactive test. Simply paste your existing MCQs (questions, options, and answers) into the box, and our tool organizes them into a clickable quiz format so you can practice in a real exam environment.

Is this tool helpful for MBBS Professionals?

Yes. It is designed specifically for medical students to practice active recall. Whether you are preparing for Modular exams, Send-ups, or your Annual Profs, solving questions here helps retain information better than passive reading.

Who creates the pre-made medical quizzes?

The quizzes are curated by Muneeb ur Rahman (Dr. Muneeb). While the collection is growing, every single question is hand-picked from standard textbooks like Gray's Anatomy and Guyton to ensure it is high-yield and exam-relevant.

About the Creator

I’m Muneeb ur Rahman, an MBBS student with a simple mission: make medical learning easier, faster, and more accessible for every student who needs it. I create and curate organised study resources—MCQs, notes, books, and high-yield summaries—and share them freely so no one has to struggle alone.

Over time, I’ve built a collection that brings together the best medical content in one place: Osmosis notes, Ninja Nerd resources, Dr. Najeeb notes, subject books, and topic-wise MCQs that I prepare and refine myself. My goal is to turn overwhelming medical content into clean, understandable, exam-ready material.
